    • Instant On Demand

    With Ignite TV, the all-in-one search including Netflix and YouTube integration makes it easy to find all your favourites in one place.1 Netflix, recorded programs, live TV – no matter what tonight’s family room feature is, experience it instantly and effortlessly with one unified search.

    Speak and be heard. The Voice Remote is the best listener in the living room. Easily search for shows, get recommendations, find entertainment by actor or director, even quote a line from a movie and watch as it magically displays.

    With Instant On Demand, you can access content as soon as it finishes airing on live TV– no matter what tonight’s family room feature is, experience it instantly and effortlessly with one unified search.2

    Personalize your entertainment experience

    • SportsApp
    • KidsZone

    Seamless Netflix integration makes it even easier to find and watch your favourites. Connect your Netflix account to Ignite TV and you can access Netflix shows and movies with a simple voice command. You don’t even have to be in the Netflix app – just search for the content you want, and it’ll appear instantly.

    Seize the play with the SportsApp, where you can set your favourite teams and leagues, record games without missing overtime, watch live games while tracking scores and more. It’s like your own personal scoreboard and front-row seats.3

    More fun for kids, less worry for parents. KidsZone on Ignite TV puts your child in command in a safe and fun digital space. Age-appropriate content and parental controls make KidsZone a perfect play room – on your TV.

    Stream your favourite music videos, tutorials and more without switching devices. Use voice commands like “Soup recipes on YouTube” or “Cat videos on YouTube” and the content you want will display instantly.

    Watch where, when and how you want.

    • Ignite TV App
    • Download & Go

    Who says you have to stream TV on your TV? Your connected devices can also be part of the IPTV experience. With the Ignite TV app, you can stream shows and watch Cloud PVR recordings from your laptops, tablets and smartphones.4

    Watch where and how you want. Store up to 200 hours of HD or 4K recordings for up to a year with Cloud PVR3, then Download & Go your favourite shows to your mobile device. So you can take your shows on the road. Or the subway. Or a plane – you get the idea.5
